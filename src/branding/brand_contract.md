# Eiger Marvel HR: Brand Contract

## Brand Positioning
Digital flagship – global, premium, trustworthy, executive, technologically advanced, human-centric, elite. Every touchpoint reinforces luxury and precision.

## Visual Language
- Editorial luxury typography (large, spaced, custom weights)
- Deep charcoal, pure white, soft neutral, professional blue accents, elegant gold highlights
- Glass morphism overlays, mesh gradients, aurora effects, layered depth
- Lucide/Phosphor iconography only
- Wide whitespace, luxury spacing, never cramped
- Layered cinematic overlays, ambient glows

## Brand Voice
- Confident, global, enterprise-level authority
- Story-driven, never generic or templated
- High-trust signals (placement stats, client logos, awards, certifications)
- Emotional engagement through premium copy, narrative flow, case studies, testimonials
- Conversion-focused, concise, modern

## Architectural Foundations
Sections:
1. Arrival (Hero)
2. Global Presence
3. Company Vision
4. Industries Served
5. Recruitment Process
6. Technology
7. Success Metrics
8. Client Trust
9. Case Studies
10. Global Talent Network
11. Call to Action

Every section choreographed for maximum perceived value and seamless brand experience.

## Motion/Interaction Identity
- Cinematic hero, GSAP scroll timelines, magnetic CTAs
- Micro-interactions: cursor glow, button ripple, elevation, navigation indicators
- Restrained, premium, always custom-timed

## Trust/Elevated Signals
- Executive-level imagery
- Compliance certifications, awards, years of expertise, industry partners
- Animated visual statistics, interactive global map, client logos

## Luxury Positioning
- Everything feels custom, never template
- Conversion-driven narrative
- Site worthy of Awwwards recognition
